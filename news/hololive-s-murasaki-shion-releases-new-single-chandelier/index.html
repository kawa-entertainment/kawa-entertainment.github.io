Hololive’s Murasaki Shion Releases New Single Chandelier

Ted
Aug 20th, 2022
•
1 min read

Murasaki Shion from Hololive has released a new single titled Chandelier. The track is produced and written by popular Vocaloid producer Kanaria, known for her tracks like KING, Identity, and Envy Baby.

The track was released in commemoration of Shion’s 4th anniversary of her debut as a VTuber. The music video’s main art was illustrated by popular VTuber illustrator LAM.

This is the third single from Murasaki Shion, following her past singles “Mage of Violet” (August 2021) and “AcaciaSyndrome” (December 2021). She also appeared in other collab singles, such as “Shiny Smily Story” (September 2019) and “Dreaming Days” (February 2021).

Shion debuted as part of Hololive’s second generation, alongside Minato Aqua, Nakiri Ayame, Yuzuki Choco and Oozora Subaru. She debuted on August 17, 2019. On April 10 this year, she hit the one million subscriber milestone on YouTube.
