AniManGaki 2022 VTuber Highlights

Jay
Aug 29th, 2022
•
2 min read

A few weeks ago, we reported that AniManGaki 2022, one of the biggest annual anime/cosplay/gaming events in Malaysia, will showcase 80 VTubers. In addition, the event also has VTubers performing on stage as part of the program. Here are some snippets from this year's event.

VTuber Showcase Area

With help from Twitch IRL streamer AGCtv, we are able to take a peek inside the dedicated area where standees of 80 Malaysia VTubers are placed at a distance from each other.

Some TV screens show clips from featured VTubers, while there's one screen that's used by select VTubers to interact with the audience inside. There's also an opportunity to buy merchandise, as well as give presents to the talents. Visitors can also take-home postcards.

Curator PSY8, who is happy to see everyone who went to the showcase enjoying their time, thanked everyone who dropped by:

TQVM everyone for stopping by #AMG2022 VTuber Showcase. There are ups & downs, it was crazy, tiring but most important, Im happy to see everyone enjoying their time & make new friends. Will have a proper thank you post but for now, time to go home.

PSY8 out! 🥰 #MYVT #MYVTUBER pic.twitter.com/RORy4mWniq
