Will talents get a new model? What happens to my old identity/model?

You will continue with your old identity/model. We plan to "adopt" our talents. Our lower royalty shares force us to focus on what's essential, and for us, that's evolving our talent's current brand. If it isn't broken, don't fix it.

Are there any gender limits for those applying?

No. Any gender can apply.

Are there any age limits for those applying?

Yes. You must be 18 years or older to apply.

Do you take royalties from memberships?

Yes. However, we take an industry-leading low of only 20% in membership royalties.

Are you not going to take a cut of donations / superchats?

We never take royalties from donations / superchats. We believe they should always go entirely to the talent.

Are there any quotas on streams?

Yes. We expect all talent to stream approximately three times a week on Youtube. Any additional streams can happen on other platforms.

I currently stream on Twitch. Can I still apply?

Yes. If selected, you will have to transfer to Youtube for future streams.

Will all talents have to move to youtube?

Yes. We've decided that we want to focus on only one platform. We chose Youtube due to our vast experience with it.

How many talents will you pick for generation 1?

We plan to sign around 3-4 talents for generation 1.

Do I have to have a certain number of followers, subscribers, or viewers?

No. However, we are looking for established content creators, so the longer you've been streaming, the better.

Will you help with stream hardware?

Yes. We have a set budget for each talent that they can use to buy hardware with a no-interest payment plan through us.

Will you help create custom tools to help make my streams more engaging?

Yes. We will create custom in-house tools and programs to serve our talent better. So they can focus on streaming.

Will you help acquire new assets to use for streams?

Yes. Our goal is to help be a buffer for our talents and reduce the amount of business-orientated stuff they do.

Will you help with sponsorships?

Yes. We plan to get our talents sponsors on their request.

Will you help with merchandise?

Yes. We will create essential merchandise for each talent. If the talent is interested in more customized merchandise, we are also happy to discuss that with them.

Will you help with market research?

Yes. On no less than a monthly basis, we will look into current trends and use our connections to consult each Talent signed.
