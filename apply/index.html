Why work with us?
We help you navigate the rough seas of content creation.
Overview
Kawa is an 'Adoption' style Vtuber agency focusing on Tech, Merchandising, and the community. We 'adopt' VTubers already streaming and seeking to nurture their brand through various perks that would be hard for an individual to procure while also connecting them with a 'Wave' of fellow content creators.
How long will this take?
This form should take you less than 30 minutes to complete (assuming you don't make a custom video). There is no save function so if you plan to do it in two parts, consider writing your answers in an external file first.
What do you offer? Glad you asked!
  • We provide Programming and Merchandising resources to every talent, as those are the two areas in which our founders have the most experience.
  • Management, video editing support, and social media advertisements are also offered to all our talents, tailored to them personally.
  • We also handle the creation and upkeep of Game servers. Plan community events and ensure our talents have the opportunity to be in convention panels and meet-ups.

The Requirements
Let's make sure you're a good fit!
We have a strict no minors policy. Countries with other age definitions (e.g., China, Korea) must be at least 19 to apply.
We expect our talents to stream a minimum of around 6-8 hours a week.
We want all our talents to use YouTube and have it make up a minimum of 1/3rd of all their stream content.
We need all our talents to stream in English primarily.
Since we are an adoption agency we require every applicant to have a fully working model that is original to them.
